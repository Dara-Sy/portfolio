About Me

Hi! My name is Dara. I graduated from Queens College with a B.A. in Economics, Cum Laude on September 2017. I come from a background in marketing where I operated brand and email campaigns of over 1.8 million contacts. During my time at Fullbeauty Brands, there was a lot of cross collaboration with the marketing and web development teams. The design and development of the company’s websites and daily emails drew my interest to web development.

I am interning as a Full-Stack Web Developer and Designer at Hunter Digital. I am a Web Development Immersive Engineering Fellow at General Assembly. I completed several projects in Express, React, and Rails. I am always eager to learn new technologies! I look forward to continue working in the Tech industry and playing an active role in a team of talented developers!
