About Me

Hi! My name is Dara. I come from a background in marketing where I operated brand and email campaigns of over 1.8 million contacts. As a marketing analyst, I worked closely with the web development team to deploy daily email blasts, which drew my interest to web development.

I am now interning as a Web Developer and Designer at Hunter Digital, where I work with numerous clients to ensure the best user experience for their websites. I conduct A/B tests on clients' ads to drive up conversion rates and boost revenue. I am always eager to learn new technologies! I look forward to continue working in the Tech industry and playing an active role in a team of talented developers!
